Notification

This part of the section shows the details of payroll-related notifications that need to be addressed by payroll officer. This includes Included in Payroll, employee’s Maturing Loan(s), employee’s Increase in Longevity Factor and employee’s Leave Commutation Request.


To access the Notification section, click the Notification tab (See Figure 3-1).

Figure 3-1 Notification page

Note: By default, notification on employees Included in Payroll is shown on the Notification page. To view notifications on Maturing Loan(s), Increase in Longevity Factor and Leave Commutation Request, refer to Sections 3.1.2 to 3.1.4.

Included in Payroll

1. Click the Included in Payroll link on the left side of the screen to view the Included in Payroll page (See Figure 3-2).

Figure 3-2 Included in Payroll page

Note: To view the Personnel Profile details of an employee included in Payroll, click the link before the corresponding employee Name in Figure 3-2.

2. To view particular employees included in payroll under the same employee type, specify the employee type from the drop-down list in Figure 3-2.

3. When done, click Go button to display the employees that matched the parameter chosen.

Maturing Loan(s)

1. Click the Maturing Loan(s) link on the left side of the screen to view the Maturing Loan(s) page (See Figure 3-3).

Figure 3-3 Maturing Loan(s) page

2. To update deduction details, click the Update icon corresponding to the employee with a maturing loan to update in Figure 3-3 to display the deduction update window on the screen (See Figure 3-4).

3. Information that can be updated includes the Loan details such as Start Date, End Date and Amount Granted, Monthly deduction amount, Period 1 deduction amount, Period 2 deduction amount and deduction Status.

Figure 3-4 Maturing Loan(s) page

4. When done, click Save button to save entry.

5. Click Cancel button to cancel the operation.

Increase in Longevity Factor

1. Click the Increase in Longevity Factor link on the left side of the screen to view the Increase in Longevity Factor page (See Figure 3-5).

Note: The New Longevity Factor depends on the employee’s First Day of Service in the Agency.

Figure 3-5 Increase in Longevity Factor page

2. To change the longevity factor of a particular employee, click the Update Factor link opposite the corresponding name of employee to update in Figure 3-5.

3. A message prompt will appear confirming changing of the selected longevity factor. Click Ok button to proceed or click Cancel button to do otherwise.

Note:Once Longevity Factor is updated there will be changes in the Longevity Factor and Longevity Pay.

Compensation

This part of the section allows user to view and update compensation details of an employee such as Personnel Profile, Income, Deduction Summary, Premium/Loans, Remittances and Tax Details.

1. To access the Compensation section, click the Compensation tab (See Figure 3-8).

Figure 3-8 Compensation page

How to Browse Employee Compensation Records

1. To start viewing employee compensation records, select the first letter of the surname of the desired employee on the alphabet navigation provided on Figure 3-8.

Note: A specific letter in the Alphabet navigation corresponds to the first letter of an employee’s surname. A link on the alphabet navigation will only be available when there is an existing record.

2. If there are multiple entries for the specific letter, click on the page numbers to move to the next entry.

Using Navigation Bar

1. To start viewing employee compensation records, select the first letter of the surname of the desired employee on the alphabet navigation provided on Figure 3-8.

Note: A specific letter in the Alphabet navigation corresponds to the first letter of an employee’s surname. A link on the alphabet navigation will only be available when there is an existing record.

2. If there are multiple entries for the specific letter, click on the page numbers to move to the next entry.

Using Search Engine

1. To select a specific employee compensation record using the search engine, type in the employee’s number or name in the Search textbox (Figure 3-8).

Note: To select employees according to Payroll Group, click the Search Box then press the Space Bar. The entries on the navigation bar will depend on the Payroll Group selected.

2. When done, click Search button to display the specific employee compensation record.

3. If there are multiple entries for the specific keyword, click on the page numbers to move to the next entry.

Permanent Employees

1. Click the Undertime/Absences button to view the Undertime/Absences window on the screen (See Figures 3-32).

Figure 3-32 Undertime/Absences for Permanent Employees window

2. For Undertime/Absences of Permanent Employees, specify the Use Data from Month and Year from the drop-down lists in Figure 3-32.

3. Click Compute button to compute the benefits/salary of permanent employees.

Figure 3-32 Undertime/Absences for Permanent Employees window

Note: A permanent employee is qualified to a Hazard Pay (which is computed as Salary times the Hazard Pay Factor of employee) provided that he/she has been present for at least 1 day, a Laundry Allowance of 22.727 multiplied to the number of days of actual service rendered and a Subsistence Allowance of 150.00 for every day present or 75.00 for services less than 4 hours.

4. To manually edit undertime/absences, click the corresponding row of the employee to edit in Figure 3-32. Information that can be updated includes the Days Absent, Hours Late and Minutes Late.

Note: After manual editing, Hazard Pay, Laundry Pay, Subsistence Allowance and Salary of permanent employees is computed.

5. When done, click Compute button to save entry.

6. Click Back icon to close the window.
