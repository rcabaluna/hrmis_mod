HR Module

The HR Module is one of DOST-CO HRMIS component that allows HR personnel to manage personal data of employees, notifies HR personnel of filed requests from employees and other admin related reminders. It also manages leave balance and daily attendance of employees. It also includes features for system maintenance, account security and reports generation.
